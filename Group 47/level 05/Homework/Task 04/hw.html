Learn About Animals

Lions

Lions are large carnivorous mammals that belong to the family of Felidae. They are known as the "king of the jungle" and are social animals that live in groups called prides. Male lions are easily recognized by their majestic manes.

Elephants

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth, known for their intelligence, strong social bonds, and long trunks. They are herbivores and play a crucial role in their ecosystems. Elephants are also known for their complex communication skills.
