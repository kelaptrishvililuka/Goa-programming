About guinea pigs

Guinea pigsare small, aquatic animals that live mainly in liquid environments. They are mostly found near the waters of the Black Sea, Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.

Guinea pigs are known for their amazing fertility and fast swimming. They form swarms and breed near water where they can protect themselves from threats.

The most common type: Some species of guinea pig, such as the harpy guinea pig, are among the most famous.
