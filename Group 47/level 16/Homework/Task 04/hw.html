Xiaomi Company

Establishment and history

Xiaomi Inc. is a Chinese multinational electronics company mainly known for manufacturing smartphones, ambient devices and other electronics. The company was founded in 2010, and its founder is Lei Jun.

Xiaomi First of all, it is known for its high-quality, but quite affordable smartphones. The company introduced innovations in both technology and sales models, such as direct-to-consumer sales.

mission: "Innovation accessible to all".

Xiaomi products

  • Mi seria: High-end smartphones that are popular all over the world.
  • Redmi seria: Mid-range smartphones that offer an excellent price/quality ratio.
  • Xiaomi Mi Band: Very popular smart bands that find users all over the world.
  • Xiaomi Mi TV: Smart TVs that offer high-quality images and unique functions.
  • Xiaomi laptops: Computers known for efficiency and price.
