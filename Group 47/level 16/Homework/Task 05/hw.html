Samsung company

Establishment and history

Samsung is a South Korean multinational company founded in 1938 by Lee Byung-chul. Originally, Samsung was a small trading company, but today it is one of the biggest technology giants in the world.

Samsung It manufactures smartphones, televisions, memory devices, household appliances and many other technological products.

mission: "Improving people's lives through innovation".

Samsung products

  • Samsung Galaxy smartphone: Premium and mid-range smartphones.
  • Samsung Smart TV: Smart TVs with high quality images.
  • Samsung Wearables: Smart watches and fitness devices.
  • Samsung Home Appliances: Refrigerators, washing machines and other household appliances.
  • Samsung Memory: Memory chips and SSDs.
