Biography

J.R.R. Tolkien (John Ronald Reuel Tolkien) was born in 1892 in England. He was of English literature Professor, philologist, and writer, though best known for his fantasy worlds, Especially as the author of The Hobbit (1937) and The Lord of the Rings trilogy (1954-1955). topics

Several central themes are found in Tolkien's works:

  • The battle between good and evil: "The Lord of the Rings" is mostly about eternity The battle between good and evil forces.
  • friendship and unity: characters coming together to achieve a common goal and Supporting each other is one of the main lines in Tolkien's works.
  • Nature and environment:Nature and environment play an important role in Tolkien's world. Which becomes clear in the forests and mountains he describes.
  • Heroism: Tolkien's characters, despite their small size, are constantly shown honor and courage.
