Cobra

Known for their venom and defensive posture with a hood, cobras are found mostly in South Asia and Africa.

Black Mamba

The Black Mamba is one of the fastest and most venomous snakes. It is native to Africa and can move up to 14 km/h.

Python

Pythons are non-venomous but extremely powerful. They constrict their prey and are commonly found in tropical regions.
