VENOM 3

About Venom Biography Personality Powers and Abilities

Venom is an alien symbiote that requires a host to bond with in order to survive. The symbiote came to Earth and first bonded with Spider-Man, enhancing his powers, but it later separated and bonded with Eddie Brock, forming the notorious antihero known as Venom. In *Venom 3*, the symbiote and Eddie Brock face new challenges, villains, and deeper emotional struggles. The movie explores the evolution of their relationship as they work together to fight a new threat to both their worlds.

In *Venom 3*, Eddie Brock faces his greatest challenge yet, as new and deadly foes threaten the safety of Earth. With the Venom symbiote pushing the limits of their bond, Eddie struggles to maintain his humanity while fighting alongside his alien counterpart. As the conflict intensifies, the line between hero and antihero becomes blurred, and Venom's true nature is tested like never before.
