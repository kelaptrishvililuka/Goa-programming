Sakamoto Days is a popular Japanese manga and anime created by Yuto Suzuki. The story follows Taro Sakamoto, a legendary hitman who leaves his life of crime behind to start a peaceful life. Main Plot: Taro Sakamoto was once an unstoppable and feared hitman. However, everything changes when he falls in love. He decides to retire from the underworld and starts living a quiet life, running a small convenience store with his family. Despite his attempts to lead a normal life, his past catches up with him, as old rivals and enemies seek him out. Anime Tone: Sakamoto Days combines comedy, action, and slice-of-life elements. It offers a mix of hilarious scenes, intense action sequences, and heartwarming moments. "Hashire Sakamoto" (translated as "Run, Sakamoto") is the opening theme of the anime. It captures the energetic and dynamic essence of the story. This anime is loved for its unique characters, sharp humor, and creative blend of action and comedy.

My Hero Academia (Boku no Hero Academia) is a highly popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Kohei Horikoshi. It is set in a world where most people possess superpowers, known as "Quirks," and follows the journey of a young boy, Izuku Midoriya, who dreams of becoming the greatest hero despite being born without a Quirk. Main Plot: Izuku Midoriya, nicknamed Deku, is a Quirkless boy who idolizes the legendary hero All Might. His life changes when All Might chooses him as his successor and passes down his Quirk, One For All. Deku enrolls in U.A. High School, a prestigious academy for training future heroes, where he and his classmates face villains, challenges, and personal growth on their paths to becoming professional heroes. Anime Tone: The series blends action, drama, and inspiration, focusing on themes like perseverance, teamwork, and what it truly means to be a hero. Official Openings: "My Hero Academia" is known for its powerful and energetic opening theme songs that hype up the audience for the intense action and emotional journey ahead. Each season introduces a new opening theme, often performed by well-known Japanese artists. These openings typically feature high-energy music paired with dynamic visuals showcasing the characters and their growth. Some notable opening themes include: "The Day" by Porno Graffitti (Season 1 Opening) "Peace Sign" by Kenshi Yonezu (Season 2 Opening) "Make My Story" by Lenny code fiction (Season 3 Opening) The openings capture the spirit of the series, inspiring fans with their motivational lyrics and epic animation.
