History

Goal-Oriented Academy is a research and development organization aimed at educating young people. Our history began in 2010 when a group of education experts established the academy to provide professional teaching and mentorship for goal-oriented learning.

Our approach is based on the individual development of students, helping them achieve their goals. Our contribution to the education of Georgian youth is an important process.

Courses

We offer a wide range of courses that help students grow in various fields.

  • Course 2: Business Strategy
  • Course 3: Leadership and Management
  • Course 4: Technology and Innovation
